# Quality Gates

Do not present work as final when it fails a required gate.

1. **Canon:** latest identity; no invented gaps; candidate not treated as canon.
2. **Anatomy:** correct hands, limbs, joints, proportions, posture, grasping, and contact; no merging; Sol's hand stays on his scythe.
3. **Identity:** face, hair, build, and presence persist; new characters are diverse; no repeated default face/body/dark outfit.
4. **Rendering:** semi-realistic painted manhwa; painterly, weathered, material, cinematic; no anime/cartoon drift or generator residue.
5. **Noise:** no particulate-heavy texture or automatic sparks/dust/debris/motes/fragments; clear hierarchy; intensity is not clutter.
6. **Geometry:** no accidental facets, crystals, shards, polygons, clothing artifacts, or default weapon constructs.
7. **Manifestation:** personal medium; motion/intent; meaningful user effect; local environmental influence; noncombat range; no monochrome attack code, stock explosion, or clutter-as-maximum.
8. **Environment:** authored architecture/material logic; history and habitation; appropriate population; no generic shorthand; dissonance has reason and internal logic.
9. **Composition:** recent shots, poses, and staging are not repeated; characters move through place; scene breathes; relationships read clearly.
10. **Comic continuity:** correct progression; no accidental repeat, constant lesson, repeated transition/activity, or unrequested subchapter; no generated text unless requested; correct lettering and speech bubble flow.
11. **Presentation:** reject visibly defective work; label candidate status; record approvals.
